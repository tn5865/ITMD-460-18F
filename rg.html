Real Grade Gundam (RG).


  • RG stands for Real-Grade.
  • Size is 1/144 scale, about 5 to 6 inches.
  • RG kits' price ranges from $20 to $45.
  • RG kits have an intermediate to advanced level of assembly.
  • These are aimed at enthusiasts.
  • They have a very high and "realistic" amount of detail
    and parts count.
